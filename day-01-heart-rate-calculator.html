Why Heart Rate Zones Matter

Training in the right heart rate zone maximizes your workout effectiveness. Each zone targets different fitness goals and energy systems in your body.

Recovery Zone (50-60% max HR): Perfect for warm-ups, cool-downs, and active recovery days. Builds aerobic base without stress.

Aerobic Zone (60-70% max HR): The "fat burning" zone. Develops cardiovascular fitness and burns fat efficiently. Ideal for longer, steady workouts.

Anaerobic Zone (70-85% max HR): Improves performance and speed. Your body switches to burning more carbohydrates. Great for tempo runs and interval training.

Max Effort Zone (85-95% max HR): High-intensity training that improves power and VO2 max. Use sparingly for short bursts during interval training.
